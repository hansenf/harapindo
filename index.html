Company Background

PT Harapindo was established in 1990. Founded by Mr. Suroso Ongkowidjojo and his partner, the company aims to be the most comprehensive solution for fulfilling industrial requirement of raw matertials. Its primary business focuses on distributing of materials for industrial process especially for textile, non woven and automotive industry. In the beginning period of establishment PT. Harapindo sells chemical process agent for fabric / yarn process to be the finished goods which is still the main business and contributes 95% from total sales.

Later in 2006, the company magnifies the growth and transforms to PT. Harap Indo Anugrah Bersatu. Granted a partneship with Sanyo Chemical Indusries Ltd, to promote and sell chemical block for design – trial production – mass production process of automotive industry. For its complimentary, adhesives of Koatsu Gas Kogyo Co., Ltd is also made as the joint distributor partnership to be unity as the chemical blocks.
