Chemical Block

SANMODUR products are tooling materials (chemical boards) developed by Sanyo Chemical, based on its own technological research in special resins and continuous production systems.

To meet various requirements as a material for models and molds used in design development, trial production and small scale production, we offer a wide range of boards.

SANMODUR products have the features required for these purposes such as fine cell structures, homogeneity, processability, light weight, strength, heat resistance, dimensional stability and little dust generation. Furthermore, some of these products with an antistatic property were developed. The dust generated from cutting is less of a problem for NC machine tools and operators.

To reduce material loss and time required for adhesion of boards, we provide boards in a wide range of thicknesses.
